Kare-kare

Kare-kare is a Philippine stew that features a thick savory peanut sauce. It is generally made from a base of stewed oxtail, beef tripe, pork hocks, calves feet, pig's feet or trotters, various cuts of pork, beef stew meat, and occasionally offal. Kare-kare is my comfort food. It was the food my mother cooks occasionally and when something special came up. I like cooking just like my mother. And I often cook this recipe to my friends.
