Bicol Express

Ingredients

  • 1 kl pork belly, diced
  • 15 pieces of Thai chili peppers, minced
  • 1 can coconut cream
  • 4 cups of coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 cups of water
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 thinly sliced meduim onion
  • 2 table spoon of shrimp paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat the pan and pour in the oil
  2. Add the onion and saute until transparent
  3. Add garlic and continue to saute until golden brown
  4. Add the pork and cook until lightly brown
  5. Add the shrimp paste and continue stirring
  6. Add water and cocout milk and continue cooking for about 35 minutes
  7. Add the coconut cream and simmer until thickened
    8. Serve with rice!
