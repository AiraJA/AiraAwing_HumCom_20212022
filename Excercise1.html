The Client

by: John Grisham



The client was the very first novel I read written by John Grisham. The book tells of a boy named Mark Sway and his little brother Ricky witnessing the suicide of a mob lawyer. Before he dies, he confides to Mark about vital information about a politician's murder. Fearing reprisal by the mob or the FBI for lying, he hires a lawyer to get him out of this mess. The story was a thriller with a humorous touch. I was thoroughly entertained for every minute of the book. The suspense was intense , and the story was good . It was a real page turner and a well written book .
